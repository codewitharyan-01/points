Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Ambassador Program

How long is the Ambassador Program? expand_more
The initial commitment is for 6 months, after which you can choose to continue based on your performance and interest. Many ambassadors stay with the program for multiple years.
Is the program paid? expand_more
While the program itself is volunteer-based, ambassadors may receive stipends for hosting specific workshops or events. Additionally, you'll receive free access to premium courses and resources.
Do I need technical expertise to apply? expand_more
No, you don't need to be an AI expert! We welcome individuals at all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. What's most important is your passion for AI and your enthusiasm for sharing knowledge.
What kind of support will I receive as an ambassador? expand_more
You'll receive comprehensive training, access to exclusive resources, mentorship from industry experts, and ongoing support from our team. We also provide materials for workshops and events.
Can I apply if I'm a student? expand_more
Absolutely! We encourage students to apply. The program can be a great way to build your resume, gain practical experience, and make valuable connections in the AI industry.

A Letter to My Love

My dearest Tulsi, my Chiku, my Tuli, my Baby,

As I sit down to write these words, my heart is overflowing with emotions that words can barely capture. Six months ago, on June 2nd, our beautiful journey began, and every single day since has been a testament to the incredible love we share.

Thank you for being the light in my life, the melody in my heart, and the peace in my soul. When I look at you, I see not just the woman I love, but my best friend, my confidant, and my greatest supporter. Your smile has the power to brighten my darkest days, and your laughter is the sweetest music I've ever heard.

In these six months, you've taught me the true meaning of love. You've shown me that love isn't just about grand gestures, but about the small, everyday moments that we share. It's in the way you listen to my stories, the way you comfort me when I'm down, and the way you celebrate my victories as if they were your own.

Thank you for your patience when I'm difficult, for your understanding when I make mistakes, and for your unwavering belief in us. You've made me a better person just by being in my life, and for that, I'll be eternally grateful.

I love the way your eyes sparkle when you talk about things you're passionate about. I love the way you get excited about the little things. I love the way you care for others, and I especially love the way you care for me.

Every moment with you feels like a beautiful dream that I never want to wake up from. You've filled my life with so much joy, laughter, and love that sometimes I wonder how I got so lucky to have you.

As we celebrate these six months together, I want you to know that my love for you grows stronger with each passing day. I promise to cherish you, to support you, to make you laugh, and to be there for you through all of life's ups and downs.

You are my today and all of my tomorrows. You are my forever.

With all my love and all my heart,

Your Aryan
